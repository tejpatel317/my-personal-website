Simple Interest Calculator

The formula for simple interest is straightforward since it does not include the effect of compounding. In other others, the interest rate will only be applied to the principal amount.

Total Amount = P + (P x R x T)

P = Principal amount in dollars

R = Anual rate of interest

T = Number of terms in years

Compound Interest Calculator

Compound interest is the interest on interest. This means that the interest is not only applied to the principal amount, but also the accumulated interest over time.

Total Amount = P(1+R/N)NT

P = Principal amount in dollars

R = Annual rate of interest

T = Number of terms in years

N = How many times interest is compounded per year
